About

Jara General Trading is a sole proprietorship type of business that was established in 2016 in accordance with commercial code of Ethiopia and registered by the federal ministry of trade of the federal democratic republic of Ethiopia. The company is one of the leading companies exporting agricultural products,minerals and other goods to different parts of the world from Ethiopia. We are also active in the import businesses. We import Construction materials, Agricultural inputs and farm implements, Vehicles and food items. Mr. Reshid Jula is the founder and over all coordinator of the company. Mr. Reshid is a well trained professional business person. Mr. Reshid has more than 18 years of experience in agricultural products marketing business, as he was the co-founder and The general manager of this company.
